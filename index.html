Welcome to Gennaro's!

We welcome you as family and a great meal is always served

My culinary journey began in '82, when I relocated to South Jersey to attend Stockton State College. I needed a part-time job to help with tuition so I started working at a local restaurant as a dishwasher and prep cook; this is where my love of creating a great meal began!

I am Joseph DeGennaro or Chef Joe if you like! I attended the Academy of Culinary Arts, and in 1987 I decided to relocate to the U.S. Virgin Islands working for two major hotel brands where I created menu items using many different types of cuisines.

I met and married my beautiful wife while living there and in 1998, after 10 years of working in the Caribbean, we decided to return to South Jersey where I became the Executive Chef at the historic Renault Winery.

To have a local restaurant and be involved in my community was always my dream! Gennaro's Italian Grille & Pizzeria, formerly, Mario's, is the perfect place for me to introduce a contemporary dining experience along with every day staple items that have sustained the former business for more than a decade. This is a place where families come together to enjoy amazing food; where we care about your dining experience and where we charge affordable prices to encourage you to join us for a meal again and again.
